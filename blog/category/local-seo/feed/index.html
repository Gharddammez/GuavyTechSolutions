The new feature allows shows users different Google Map Business deals and events that are happening in the vicinity.

It displays promotions, deals, and events from various businesses’ Google Business Profile posts, popping up in your local searches.

What is Google Nearby Events & Deals Feature? 

The feature is great for offering local businesses a chance to boost conversions. If you want to appear on Google Nearby Events & Deals and your business happens to have a Google Business Profile, posting regularly and optimizing your GMB posts will get you a chance to appear in this section. 

When you search for your desired query, such as “best breakfast in Nairobi” or “best brunch in Toronto,” the feature will filter the best results and show any nearby events or happening deals. 

These results typically appear lower on the mobile SERP, positioned beneath the local pack, organic results, ‘People also ask,’ and ‘People also search for.’ 

The feature appears in four tiles with the company title, a promotional photo, and a review rating. If you see something you like, Google takes you to a full-screen view of the offer.

Here, you will find the business name, its location on Google Maps, and a visually appealing image of the promotion. You will also find a small descriptive text that will discuss its features. 

How Does Google Nearby Event Feature Work? 

Google collects information from a list of websites that have a reputation for collecting news on events. Some of these sites are Ticketmaster, Eventbrite, SeatGeek, Bookmyshow.com, Allevents.in, LiveNation, Meetup, and many others. 

As per Google, the list of these nearby event websites often changes depending on relevance.

Once the event is coming up or there’s a lucrative deal on any product offerings or service offerings, Google will automatically pull up GMB profiles of the business and showcase the event or deal on relevant keyword searches for the user. 

Why Is This Feature Important for Local Businesses? 

Many local businesses always run enticing deals or events that they want to promote to increase business. When these deals and events start showing up on local searches, it becomes easier for them to reach out to their previous existing customers and new ones alike. 

If you’re a local business owner planning to sell your product/service online, and you have come up with a deal or hosting an event, Google Nearby Events & Deals can provide you with a chance to make it visible. 

As a local business, it’s more than mandatory to keep posting for GMB profiles regularly, especially if you have year-end or holiday season deals planned and ready for dispatch. 

Note: This feature is exclusive to local searches on mobile devices and has not been observed on Google Maps.

How Can Businesses Optimize Their Listings for Nearby Events & Deals? 

To ensure your events appear on Google:

  1. Check if your event publisher integrates with Google search. If so, continue posting events on their platform.
  2. If you use a CMS like WordPress without HTML access, find a plugin to add structured data or use Google’s Data Highlighter.
  3. If experienced with HTML editing, directly integrate with Google using structured data on your event pages.

How To Show Up In Nearby Events & Deals?

For optimal performance of your event structured data:

  1. Ensure the inclusion of required and recommended properties as per our developer guidelines.
  2. Maintain high-quality event details; utilize the description field to elaborate on the event rather than duplicate the title, date, and location.
  3. Utilize the Rich Result Test to validate and preview your structured data.

What Are the Potential Benefits and Drawbacks of this Feature? 

There are benefits and drawbacks to investing your precious time and money in Google Nearby Events and deals. 

Benefits of Google Nearby Events & Deals

  • Increased Visibility – Most local businesses gain exposure to a broader audience by having events coming up in relevant local search options. 
  • Better User Engagement – Local users will start seeing local events and deals to develop the latest business offerings and appealing promotions. 
  • Local Brand Promotion – Many local businesses can strengthen local brand presence by constantly appearing in nearby local event listings. 
  • Greater Audience Reach – Many events appear on user proximity and search intent, ensuring businesses reach users who are likely interested in such offerings. 
  • Competitive Advantage – Nearby Events & deals can offer businesses an edge over competition by attracting more foot traffic and achieving customer interest. 

Drawbacks of Google Nearby Events & Deals 

  • Reliance on Third-Party Websites – Businesses can have limited control over the event listings, as most of them are present on external event platforms. 
  • Potential Inaccuracy – There’s always a risk of displaying outdated or incorrect event details, especially when the information is on third-party websites. 
  • Limited Exposure – Not all businesses may be eligible to appear or get featured in Nearby Event listings, limiting their exposure to local establishments. 
  • Competition for Attention – When multiple businesses start vying for user attention in nearby event listings, it can make it challenging for your business to stand out. 
  • Dependency on Google Algorithms – Changes in Google’s algorithm can lead to fluctuations in business exposure. 

Note: It is important to highlight that the Nearby Events & Deals feature, as per our testing, only shows up for food and restaurant businesses and is currently showing up for US based local searches.

Businesses can actively participate in nearby event listings, connect with local communities, and drive foot traffic.

]]> Optimizing Your Website for Local Search: Essential Tips for Business Owners https://guavytechsolutions/blog/local-seo-tips-small-business-owners/ Wed, 14 Jun 2023 14:09:03 +0000 https://guavytechsolutions/?p=12109 Are you a business individual looking to attract more local customers? Overview of Local Search Optimization

While word-of-mouth is still the finest form of marketing, what happens when potential customers search for your products or services online? To ensure that your website appears in local search results, optimize it for local search.

Benefits of Optimizing Your Website for Local Search

As a business owner, optimizing your website for local search is essential to improve your chances of being found by potential customers in your area. There are several benefits of optimizing your website for local search, including:

  1. Increased Visibility: Optimizing your website for local search can help increase your visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs), making prospective customers more likely to find and click on your listing.
  2. Greater Reach: Localized keywords can help you reach a wider audience of potential customers within your geographic region.
  3. More Targeted Traffic: By targeting specific keywords and phrases related to your location, you can attract visitors more likely to be interested in your offer.
  4. Improved ROI: Optimizing your website for local search can help improve your return on investment (ROI) by generating higher-quality leads more likely to convert into paying customers.

Essential Local SEO Steps to Optimize Your Website for Local Search

Here are some essential steps you can take to optimize your site for local search:

  1. Add Your Business Listing to Online Directories

Ensure your business is listed on popular online directories, such as Google My Business, Yelp, and Foursquare. It will help improve your visibility in local search results. 

  1. Optimize your Website for Local Keywords

Use local keywords in your website content to help enhance your ranking in local search results. For example, if you’re a Los Angeles plumber, use relevant keywords like “plumbing Los Angeles” or “Los Angeles plumbing services” throughout your site. 

  1. Create Local Content

In addition to using local keywords, create content targeting your local audience. Write blog posts about local news and events, or make videos highlighting your services in specific cities or regions.

  1. Promote Your Website Locally

Ensure your website is promoted locally through offline channels such as print ads, flyers, and word-of-mouth. You can also elevate your site through online channels such as social media and online directories/listings.

Building Links to Your Site

Local search is a powerful way to attract an audience for small businesses. It allows customers to find your business more easily online in local searches and can help you to stand out from the competition.

One of the most essential things you can do to optimize your website for local search is to build links to your site on local online platforms. It is because links are one of the fundamental factors that search engines use to determine the ranking of websites in their search results.

There are several ways to build links to your website, including:

  • Submitting your website to local directories and listings sites.
  • Reaching out to local businesses and asking for link exchanges.
  • Participating in local community events and activities.
  • Creating informative and shareable content on your website (such as blog posts, infographics, etc.)

Building links to your website can be time-consuming, but it is well worth the effort. Not only will it help improve your website’s ranking in local search results, but it will also help drive traffic and brand awareness.

Leveraging Google My Business Listings

Google My Business (GMB) listing is one of the most critical factors in determining your ranking in local search results.

That’s why optimizing your GMB listing for local search is critical. Here are a few local SEO tips:

  1. Ensure your NAP (name, address, and phone number) is correct and up-to-date.
  2. Add photos and videos to showcase your business.
  3. Include relevant keywords in your business description.
  4. Encourage customers to leave reviews on your GMB listing.
  5. Respond to all reviews, both positive and negative.

By following the above-stated steps, you can help ensure that your GMB listing is optimized for local search and that potential customers can easily find you when they search for businesses like yours online.

Measuring Results and Making Adjustments

As a business owner, it’s essential to track how well your website is performing in local search. It can be done by measuring your website’s organic traffic and conversion rate.

If you notice that your website is getting less traffic than you’d like or that your traffic needs to convert into customers, you’ll need to make some adjustments.

By tracking your results and making the necessary adjustments, you can ensure that your website is visible to prospective customers in your local area looking for what you offer.

What is Local SEO?

Local SEO is a popular practice where you optimize your content on relevant area-based search terms so your content can start appearing on the top searches in that particular region.

It helps increase your online visibility, helps others easily find your stuff online within your vicinity, and gives you more authenticity in the online world.

Local Pack, Finder, Organic Search Results & Google Maps

Local Pack is the specific area that appears on top of the search engine result page but right below the Google Search bar. It contains the listings with pinpoint locations on the map of your desired local business marking the exact location where your business is present.

Local Finder is a location-based feature offered by Google Search which helps you identify your business, whether it appears in the Local Pack of Google or not. Beneath the Local Finder is the Localized Organic Result, which usually appears like every other website in Google Search.
